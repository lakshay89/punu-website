Our Servcies

Occupational Therapy

Our experienced OTs help to maximise your independence through effective, evidence-based interventions.

Speech Pathology

We support adults and children with speech impairments including language, literacy, communication and swallowing.

Positive Behaviour Support

Our Practitioners are trained in evidence-based strategies to help reduce or eliminate behaviours of concern.

Physiotherapy

Our physiotherapists can help you improve movement and function, and make tasks easier.

Exercise Physiology

Tailored exercise programs to help you safely and effectively self-manage your condition.

Psychology

We provide intervention and support for mental health, behavioural and emotional conditions.

Early Childhood Approach

We support children under nine with disability or developmental delays to get the best start in life.

School Leaver Employment Support

An early intervention approach to build job skills and support your employment success.

Employment-related Assessment and Counselling

This service will establish your best pathway to successfully engage in employment.

Functional Capacity Assessments

A service to assess the right level of support and funding to achieve your unique goals.

Med health
Ability Action Australia is part of MedHealth

MedHealth supports thousands of people each year to achieve better work and health outcomes. We do this through our unique combination of strengths, capabilities and resources which span employment, medical opinion, advisory, rehabilitation and return to work services. Our 3,000 strong team of committed professionals use their talents to help others realise their potential. Backed with the expertise of more than 1,200 independent medical specialists, we cover all major medical and allied health disciplines. Delivering localised expertise in more than 300 locations across Australia, we are wherever our customers need us most, when they need us.
